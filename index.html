Our Story

Blooms crafted with intention & heart

Juno's Flowerpatch was born from a deep love of nature's quiet beauty — the way a single stem can transform a room, how a bouquet can carry a feeling words can't quite hold.

Every arrangement is made to order using fresh, seasonal flowers chosen for their colour, texture, and soul. Whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply bringing a little beauty into your everyday, we pour care into every petal.

Oh, and this is our Chief Floral Inspector — always on hand to supervise, smell the blooms, and occasionally collect a bug or two. 🐾
